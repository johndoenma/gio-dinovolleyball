April 16th–19th at the Kauai Royal Sonesta Beach Volleyball Courts

Our annual beach volleyball event is dedicated to the Kauaʻi community. Come share a fun-filled day creating memories with old friends and making new connections on the courts!

A story 32 years in the making.

The start of an "old school" tradition.

In 1994, founders Marin Artukovich and David Chaikin created the Hawaii Dinosaur Volleyball tournament to give older players a platform for high-level "old school" beach volleyball.Decades later, this cherished tradition continues to draw players for fierce competition and family fun at its beloved home on Kalapaki Beach, Kauai.

Beyond the net, this event serves a higher purpose within the community. Proceeds generated from the tournament are proudly dedicated to supporting local volleyball teams and youth groups. By participating, you are directly helping to further the sport, ensuring the next generation of players on Kauaʻi has the resources and opportunities they need to thrive.

Spike like a master!

Competition is open to everyone, from our youngest Keiki to the toughest "Dino" Masters! Beyond the legendary high-age combined divisions, we invite family teams-like the popular Father/Son and Mother/Daughter brackets-to compete in a spirited environment that embraces all generations of beach volleyball players.

Tournament Dates

April 16-19th, 2025

The tournament spans four intense days of "old school" beach volleyball, structured around double-elimination brackets for various age-group divisions.

Matches are played on a large court using side-out scoring, where the losing team immediately remains on court to referee the next game, ensuring a fast-paced schedule.
